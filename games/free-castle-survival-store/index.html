Ready to Run a Store in the Dead Zone?

“Free Castle: Survival Store” is a first person store simulator in a post apocalyptic zombie invasion era.

Build, trade, clean, fight—and survive.

Survival Store mixes simulation and chaos in a gritty post-apocalyptic setting.

Every task you complete earns trust. Every mess you leave and every action you take might cost you everything.

Can you keep the business alive when the world’s falling apart?

Loot, haggle, and build your rep.

Scavenge goods, bargain with stubborn survivors, and boost your reputation by keeping them alive—and satisfied.

Build your outpost from scratch.

Place furniture, set up defensive structures, and organize your survivor hub with total freedom.

They hear you. They see you.

Make a sound, and they’ll come. Get spotted, and they’ll chase.

Waves of undead. Endless bloodshed.

Face massive hordes in brutal combat. Use melee, guns, and traps to survive the swarm—or get swallowed by it.

Vehicles are your lifeline.

Customize, fuel, and drive vehicles to transport goods or escape the undead hordes.

Scrub the blood. Clean the grime. Earn their respect.

Disinfect walls, floors, and surfaces to keep your store safe, usable, and infection-free.
A spotless store isn’t just cosmetic—it directly boosts your reputation and customer trust.

Filth piles up. You clean it up.

Pick up scattered junk, bag the waste, and keep your store from turning into a post-apocalyptic landfill. Trash-free zones boost your reputation and keep customers coming.

Repaint your store’s walls to cover the mess, mark your territory, or just feel human for a second.
